FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team directly.

How long does it take to set up InventoHub?
Most teams are up and running within 24–48 hours. Our onboarding specialists guide you through every step, from data migration to workflow configuration. Complex setups with multiple warehouses may take up to a week.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime during or after the trial period.
Can InventoHub integrate with my existing systems?
Absolutely. We offer native integrations with SAP, Oracle, Shopify, WooCommerce, QuickBooks, and 50+ other platforms. Our REST API also allows custom integrations with virtually any system.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, phone support for Enterprise plans, and a comprehensive knowledge base with video tutorials. Enterprise clients also get a dedicated account manager.
Is my data secure with InventoHub?
Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, and are SOC 2 Type II certified. Our infrastructure runs on AWS with multi-region redundancy and automated backups every 6 hours.
