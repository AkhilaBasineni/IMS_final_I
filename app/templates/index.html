System Architecture

How Data Flows Through InventoHub

Observe how data seamlessly flows through the InventoHub nodes.

1. Procurement

Managers generate Purchase Orders. Upon vendor delivery, goods are instantly queued for warehouse intake via the logistics module.

2. Core Inventory

Products populate localized Warehouses. The central ledger calculates exact Live Stock, replenishment points, and auto-flags stockouts.

3. Fulfillment

Sales Orders are logged. When marked "Shipped", the engine automatically deducts units from live stock — zero manual entry required.

4. Business Intel

Every transaction updates the Executive Dashboard. Role-based views show real-time Stock Valuation and revenue metrics.
